Under the Sea Typography Poster

Self-Study 2017

This typography poster was created in Adobe Photoshop. It is an actual picture of me right before I went scuba diving in the Mediterranean Sea off an island in Greece. The object of the assignment was to use typography from lyrics of a song. I thought that the “Under the Sea” song from The Little Mermaid movie was appropriate for this image. I made the word “floating” look like it was drifting off. Additionally, I used a picture of mermaid scale fabric as the inside filling of the title and credits of the song.
